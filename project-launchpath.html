Project details

LaunchPath Studio landing page.

A focused business landing page designed to explain a product strategy service and guide visitors toward booking a consultation.

Overview

LaunchPath had strong expertise but a vague web presence. The page needed to communicate who they help, what the engagement includes, and why a founder should trust them.

Project facts

  • Type: Landing page
  • Role: Content structure, UI design, front-end build
  • Tools: HTML and CSS
  • Result: clearer offer story and stronger CTA path

Challenge

The service was valuable but abstract. Visitors needed simple sections that turned strategy language into clear business benefits.

Design decisions

I used a strong hero, proof cards, process steps, service outcomes, and FAQ content to answer doubts before the final CTA.

Outcome

The page became easier to scan, more persuasive, and more reusable for future launch offers.
