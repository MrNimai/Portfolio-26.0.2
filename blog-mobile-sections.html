Designing mobile sections first.

Small screens reveal whether your content order and hierarchy actually work.

When a section works on mobile, it usually becomes stronger on desktop too. The limited width forces you to choose what matters most.

Prioritize the message

Lead with the clearest heading and supporting text. Avoid making users scroll through decoration before reaching the point.

Stack with intention

Cards, columns, and image-text layouts should stack in an order that still tells a good story.

Keep tap targets comfortable

Buttons and links should be easy to press. Mobile design is not only about fitting content; it is about making the page comfortable to use.
