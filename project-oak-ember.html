Project details

Oak & Ember e-commerce concept.

A premium home-goods shopping concept with calm browsing, clear filters, and product details that support confident decisions.

Overview

This concept explored how a small e-commerce brand could feel refined without overwhelming shoppers. The page uses editorial sections, product cards, and simple buying signals.

Project facts

  • Type: E-commerce UI concept
  • Role: Product-card design and page layout
  • Tools: HTML and CSS
  • Result: premium browsing with practical clarity

Challenge

Product pages needed to feel tasteful while still making details, pricing, and trust signals easy to find.

Design decisions

I used clean product grids, collection stories, soft borders, and compact filters that do not dominate the page.

Outcome

The concept balances brand atmosphere with clear shopping behavior and responsive product browsing.
