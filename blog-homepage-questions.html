What your homepage needs to answer.

A homepage should quickly reduce confusion and show the visitor where to go next.

Your homepage does not need to say everything, but it should answer the most important questions before the visitor loses interest.

Who do you help?

Make the audience clear. A visitor should know whether your work is relevant to them within a few seconds.

What do you do?

Use direct language. Explain the service or skill without hiding behind vague creative phrases.

Why should someone trust you?

Show selected work, testimonials, process, results, or experience. Trust grows when claims are supported by evidence.
